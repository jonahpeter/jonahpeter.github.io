PHYSICAL MODELS FOR THE ORIGIN OF LIFE

The idea that the earliest stages of chemical evolution may have proceeded deterministically as a consequence of astrophysical and planetary constraints is both intuitive and appealing. I study the physical processes leading to the formation of life's essential building blocks: from charged particle dynamics on the surfaces of icy worlds, to the quantum optical properties of primordial biopolymers.

QUANTUM COHERENCE: AN EXPLANATION FOR BIOLOGICAL HOMOCHIRALITY?

The pervasive observation of enantiomeric excesses consistent with Earth biology (right-handed sugars, left-handed amino acids) amongst compositionally diverse bodies across the solar system points towards an astronomical scale symmetry breaking event before the origin of life.
